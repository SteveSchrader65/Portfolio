The Toy Shop Story

The Toy Shop was founded back in 1992 when the owner of a small, suburban toy store branched out and acquired a second business on the other side of Perth which he then built into a cross-city brand. Since then, owner Peter Fyle has gone from strength to strength, growing the business into an Australia-wide phenomenon.

Peter describes himself as someone who simply just never grew up. As he explains, here at the Toy Shop it is our belief that only kids can truly know what other kids will like. We make it our goal to play with your children.

We also employ teams of kids whose sole purpose is to stress-test the latest toys from around the globe. Our philosophy is that if they can’t break them, then we put them on our shelves.

Under Peter’s patronage our toy-experts regularly tour the European and US markets taking along groups of 8yo-13yo children and doing the rounds of the international toy conventions in search of that latest craze.

And, so you, Dear Parent, can know that your child’s future is safe in our hands.
